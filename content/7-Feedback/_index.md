### INTERNSHIP EVALUATION & REVIEW REPORT: AWS VIETNAM
**Company:** Amazon Web Services Vietnam Company Limited (AWS Vietnam)  
**Role:** Cloud Engineer
**Duration:** [(From April 17 2026 - July 10 2026)]  
**Mentor/Manager:** Nguyen Gia Hưng 

---

### 1. Company Overview
Amazon Web Services Vietnam is the official subsidiary of AWS – the world’s most comprehensive and broadly adopted cloud platform.
* **Work Environment:** Imbued with Amazon's global culture, driven by the "Day 1" mentality (treating every day as the first day of a startup) and guided by the famous **16 Leadership Principles**.
* **Office:** Modern, professional, and fully accommodating a creative and flexible Hybrid working model.
* **The Team:** Composed of leading experts and solutions architects in the cloud industry who are exceptionally approachable and always willing to share knowledge, reflecting Amazon's "Peculiar" culture.

---

### 2. Internship Objectives
* **Technical Knowledge:** Deepen understanding of core AWS services (Compute, Storage, Database, Security, Networking) and stay updated with modern technology trends such as *Serverless, AI/ML (Amazon Bedrock), and DevOps*.
* **Professional Skills:** Learn how to analyze real-world business challenges and design robust system architectures aligned with the **AWS Well-Architected Framework**.
* **Soft Skills:** Sharpen critical thinking, practice professional English communication in a multinational environment, and cultivate customer-facing skills and effective teamwork.

---

### 3. Tasks & Projects Completed
Throughout the internship, I was entrusted with practical responsibilities and hands-on projects, including:
* **Research & Internal Training:** Completed technical learning paths and achieved industry-recognized AWS professional certifications (e.g., *AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner* or *AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate*).
* **Hands-on Project Development:** Successfully built a Proof of Concept (PoC) for the **"AWS Helpdesk Portal"**—an automated IT ticket management system utilizing a 100% Serverless architecture (API Gateway, Lambda, DynamoDB, SQS, SES) integrated with a fully automated CI/CD pipeline to optimize operations.
* **Solutions Architecture (SA) Team Support:** Assisted in preparing technical documentation, drafting technical proposals, and researching case studies of major enterprise clients in Vietnam to evaluate cost optimization and security enhancement strategies.

---

### 4. Self-Evaluation

### 📈 Achievements & Strengths
* **Technical Growth:** Mastered the foundational mindset of designing highly scalable, faults-tolerant, and secure distributed systems on the cloud (both encryption-at-rest and in-transit).
* **Cultural Alignment:** Internalized and effectively applied core leadership principles into daily problem-solving, most notably *Customer Obsession* and *Bias for Action*.
* **Successful Project Delivery:** Delivered a stable Serverless demo that is highly cost-efficient (estimated at only ~$26/month for 50,000 requests), receiving positive reviews and appreciation from the AWS Mentor Panel.

### 🔍 Areas for Improvement
* **Deep Technical Troubleshooting:** Requires more exposure and time to master advanced debugging and log analysis in complex, large-scale microservices environments.
* **Commercial Presentation Skills:** Simplifying highly intricate technical jargon into concise, value-driven business language tailored for C-level executives remains a skill I need to practice further.

---

### 5. Feedback on Environment & Mentorship
* **Mentorship & Team Support:** My mentor possessed profound technical expertise and provided dedicated, structured weekly syncs to guide both my project progress and career roadmap. The broader engineering team at AWS Vietnam was welcoming, completely eliminating any hierarchical barrier between interns and seniors.
* **Growth Opportunities:** AWS provides an infinite pool of learning resources (AWS Skill Builder, Qwiklabs, and sandbox accounts with AWS credits for free experimentation). It serves as the ultimate launchpad for anyone aspiring to build a career in Cloud Computing.

---

### 6. Conclusion & Future Aspirations
This internship at **AWS Vietnam** has been an invaluable journey that effectively bridged the gap between academic theory and the rigorous, fast-paced demands of the global IT industry. I have emerged from this program equipped with the confidence, technical foundation, and soft skills needed to enter the professional tech workforce.

**Future Aspirations:** Upon graduation, my ultimate goal is to return to AWS Vietnam as a full-time employee through global talent pipelines such as the *AWS Tech U* program or by applying directly for associate solutions architect/cloud engineer roles, aiming to empower Vietnamese enterprises with cloud technology.
